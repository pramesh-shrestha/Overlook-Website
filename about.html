About Us

We areThe Overlook Hotel

We are a modest, traditional family-owned hotel with a high level of luxury. Our motto has been the same since our establishment in the 17th century: "Come as a Guest, Live as Family." We welcome visitors from all over the world who come to experience our rich history and tradition, which can be seen from the rooms to the exterior.

For decades, we have managed to build the same traditions that identify us. At Overlook Hotel, we strive to be the first choice in the mind of guests,owners and talent. In our journey to achieve this, we practice strong beliefs and actions that respect the diversity of people, the community, ethics and the planet. Our team is committed to extra thoughtful care and are passionate with delivering an amazinghotel experience.
